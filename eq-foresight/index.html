What It Does

🔮

30-Minute Prediction

CoreML cascade of 4 models predicts hypoglycemia at 5, 10, 15, and 30-minute horizons — entirely on-device.

📊

Real-Time Dashboard

Live glucose chart with a dotted prediction line and confidence bands. See where you're heading, not just where you are.

🔔

Smart Alerts

Escalating notifications (warning → alarm → pump stop) with intelligent cooldowns and night-mode sensitivity.

🏃

Activity Mode

Automatically adjusts glucose targets during exercise to prevent workout-induced lows. Post-exercise monitoring included.

🌙

Morning Briefing

Automated overnight analysis: "Your night was stable. Lowest point 4.2 mmol/L at 03:15."

🎛

Auto-Tune

Analyzes 24 hours of data to suggest personalized insulin sensitivity and carb ratio adjustments.

📱

Widget & Live Activity

Current glucose, risk trend, and quick-launch on your Lock Screen, Home Screen, and Dynamic Island.

🧠

Privacy-First AI

All inference runs locally. No cloud. No subscription. Your health data never leaves your device.

🔒 Privacy & Ethics

  • All AI inference runs on-device. No server, no cloud processing.
  • No automated dosing. The app warns and recommends, but never controls insulin.
  • Open Nightscout integration. Users own their data.
  • No data monetization. No tracking. No analytics without consent.
